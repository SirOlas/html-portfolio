The Best Movies According to Olas

My top 3 movies of all time.

Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind

This is a love story unlike any other. It features Jim Carrey and Kate Winslet in 2004.

Across the Universe

The entire movie is beatles songs re-imagined in a musical style. It takes place during the Vietnam war era and follows a young man from Liverpool that is trying to find his father. He ends up meeting a friend that changes the course of his life.

Dead Poet's Society

Robin Williams and Ethan Hawke are featured in this 1989 film. The story is about learning to be true to yourself and your dreams. Carpe Diem.
